# Notes on Statistics 

## Definitions 

* *Standard Normal Distribution*: mean = 0, standard_deviation = 1.
* *General Normal Distribution*: A standard normal distribution, that has been translated by a non-zero or stretched by a standard deviation != 1. In other words, it has 0 skew -- isn't leaning to one side or the other -- and 0 kurtosis -- it isn't too pointy or too plush.
